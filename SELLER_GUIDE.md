# SolarPunk Market — Seller Guide

## How to List

1. Open a GitHub Issue using the [List My Store](.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/list-my-store.md) template
2. We add your card to `index.html` within 24 hours
3. Your store is live

## Fee Structure

| Fee | Amount | Who takes it |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| Platform fee | **0%** | SolarPunk Market takes nothing |
| Card processing (unavoidable) | 2.9% + $0.30 | Visa/Mastercard network |
| PayPal (if you use it) | 2.9% + $0.30 | PayPal |
| Bitcoin / Lightning | ~0.01% | Bitcoin network |
| Cash / bank transfer | 0% | Nobody |

**You keep: 97.1% of every card sale. 100% of every Bitcoin/cash sale.**

## Payment Options

You control your own payments. Buyers pay you directly. Options:
- **PayPal.me link** — simplest, instant setup, 2.9% fee
- **Stripe** — professional, requires business account
- **Gumroad** — handles digital delivery, takes 10% (they take a cut, your choice)
- **Bitcoin** — add a BTC address, zero platform fee, small network fee
- **Lightning** — instant, near-zero fee, needs Strike/Wallet of Satoshi
- **Cash** — Venmo, CashApp, Zelle — no fees, local buyers

## Cause Transparency

You don't have to donate any %. But if you do, we put it on your listing card.
Buyers respond to it. It's not required. It's just true.

## What We Won't List

- Anything illegal
- Surveillance tools, weapons, anything that hurts people
- Scams, fake goods, plagiarized work

Everything else: yes. All causes. All media. All price points.
